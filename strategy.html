Marketing Strategy

We build you a hub — a website, a story video, and a podcast — that tells your story, proves you're the authority in your niche, and is built to turn the referrals and contacts you already have into booked conversations. A foundation that does the convincing before you ever get on the phone.

The strategy

Your growth already runs on referrals and relationships. The catch is that all of it rides on you being in the room — and when someone you're referred to looks you up, what they find has to match what they were told. So we build you a hub and point everything at it. The goal of the website:

Tell your story
Who you are, who you serve, why you.
Demonstrate authority
Proof you're the one for this specific client — not a generalist.
A place to send people
Every referral, every contact, every "go check them out" lands somewhere that sells for you.
Generate leads
A visitor raises their hand (a marketing-qualified lead), then books a call (a sales-qualified lead).

The website is the center of gravity. The story video and the podcast feed it.

How the three pieces do the work

The whole strategy is simple: speak to your prospect's problem and pain, then present your solution — in the format that actually lands. Each asset has a job.

The website
The hub. Names your prospect's problem in their own words, proves your authority, and gives them one clear next step. Everything points here.
The story video
The trust. People believe a person, not a page. Your story and your face carry a credibility a paragraph never could — a referred prospect shows up already warmer.
The podcast
The authority. Eight episodes that speak straight to your niche's real problems, pains, and questions, and walk the listener to your solution — on their time, not yours. Recorded once; working for you long after.

“I've got a vague problem” → “they understand it” → “they're clearly the one” → “I want to talk.”

Phase 1 — the foundation

Website — the hub
Your story, your positioning, and the pieces that turn interest into a conversation: a fit assessment that captures a lead, and call booking built right in, so a prospect can raise their hand and get on your calendar without waiting.
Story video
A firm story, plus a short one for each partner.
Static podcast
Eight episodes mapped to your niche, built from the research.

This is the foundation. It's built to stand on its own — and to have more added once it's proving out.

The foundation comes first. The rest is there when you want it.

Next steps

  1. Kickoff & inputs. You send current site access, any brand assets, a few examples of sites you dislike (as useful as ones you like), a few bullets on your ideal client, and the name of your compliance point-person.
  2. Research. We build the ICP brief — your clients' real problems, pains, and the language they use.
  3. Pages & wireframe. We determine the pages your site needs and lay out the structure for you to react to.
  4. Branding. Logo/wordmark, palette, type, the overall look.
  5. Web page copy. We draft; you shape the voice and confirm the facts.
  6. Schedule the story video shoot. Lock a date and location.

From there we build, QA, and launch — site live in about 45 days — with the podcast recorded around the shoot.
